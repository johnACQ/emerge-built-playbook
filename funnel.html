Hero headline

"Your backyard is now legal to build on. Find out what your lot qualifies for, and what your suite would cost, free, in 3 minutes."

Sub: A second home built in our factory and craned into place. Livable in weeks, not years. Licensed, insured, and CSA-approved so the city doesn't re-inspect.

The 3 outcomes

  • Family, close by. A private home for your parents or your kids, on your land, without sharing a roof.
  • Income that pays you back. A legal suite can rent for $2,000-$3,200 a month and adds real equity to your home.12
  • Done in weeks. 85-90% is built in our factory while your site is prepped, then craned in. One crew, in and out.

Why Emerge (reasons to believe)

  • 27 years and 20+ modular projects behind us.
  • 40+ patents on how our units are built and connected.
  • CSA-approved factory: the city accepts the factory stamp, one drain inspection on site and you're done.
  • Built in Alberta. Local steel, local crew, no ocean freight, no surprises.

B

The 90-second VSL script

Shot on your quarterly factory day. Caleb or Greg to camera, plain and direct, the way you talk. Least words, least time, sell the outcome.

[0:00 · Hook] "If you own a home in Alberta or BC, the law just changed, and almost nobody's told you. Your backyard is probably now legal to build a second home on."

[0:12 · Problem] "Maybe you've got parents who need to be close but want their own space. Maybe your kid can't afford to move out. Maybe you just want a suite that pays you rent every month. The problem has always been the same: building takes forever, trades are impossible to get, and the cost is a mystery."

[0:35 · Mechanism] "We're Emerge. We build homes in a factory. 85 to 90% of your suite is finished indoors while your site gets prepped, then we crane it into place in a day. CSA-approved, so the city doesn't crawl all over it. You get a finished, inspected home in weeks, not years."

[0:55 · Proof] "27 years, 40-plus patents, built right here in Alberta. Not shipped from overseas."

[1:08 · Offer + CTA] "Put your address in below and we'll tell you free, in about three minutes, what your lot qualifies for and what your suite would cost. And if money's the worry, there's an $80,000 government loan at 2% we'll walk you through. Enter your details and let's see what you can build."

The qualifying questions (scored in the CRM)

  • Motivation family / rental income / resale / small multiplex (routes them to the right pitch)
  • Lot address confirmed, own vs considering buying
  • Timeline ready now / 3-6 months / just exploring
  • Budget comfort aware of the $150K-$300K+ range, and open to the financing conversation

Your closer only ever talks to someone who owns a real lot, has a real reason, and knows the ballpark. No tire-kickers.
