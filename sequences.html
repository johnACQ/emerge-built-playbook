The 30-second call script (AI or human)

Open: "Hi Sarah, this is Emerge Built calling you straight back about the suite you were looking at, is now an okay time for two minutes?"

Confirm: "I've got your address as [address], is that the lot you'd build on?"

Qualify: "Quick one, is this more for family, for rental income, or a resale project?" then "And are you looking to move in the next few months or still exploring?"

Book: "Great, the next step is a 15-minute call with our team to show you exactly what your lot qualifies for and the cost. I've got today at 3 or tomorrow at 10, which suits?"

If no answer: voicemail drop + the text above fire automatically, then the follow-up sequence below picks it up.
